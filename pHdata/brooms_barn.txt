Soil Survey of Brooms Barn Experimental Station.
Sample data for topsoil (0-20 cm) bulked over supports 
of 16 m x 16 m at intervals of 40 m on a square grid.

Easting (column), Northing (row), 
exchangeable potassium (K) in mg per litre of soil, log10 K, pH, 
available phosphorus (P) also in mg per litre, and log10 P.

Missing values are indicated by -9.0.
